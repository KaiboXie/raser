# Distributed Research Scheme 

[[史欣]]

## Scope of orgnization structure 
- Team Leader 
	- Scope: direction, resource, training 
	- Contact: projects leaders 
	- Report: Team, Funding Agency 
- Project Leader 
	- Scope: project progress, deliverable, risk, publication
	- Contact: developers 
	- Report: Team Leader 
- Develper
	- Scope: research tasks, skills, research paper 
	- Contact: resource from Team  
	- Report: Project Leader 
	  

## In Practice 

- Team Leader 
	- Orgnize staff meetings (monthly or bi-monthly) 
		- Input: funding, people
		- Output: paper, product 
	- Orgnize weekly team meetings 
		- Direction: news and plan 
		- Projects status and review (merge requests from project leader)
		- Training: Journal Club, Knowledge Base, RASER Weeks 
			- (Slides-mode in OB to practice presentation skills) 
	
- Project Leader
	- Orgnize project meetings (weekly or daily)
		- Input: developers status and review 
		- Output: progress report on REPs 
		- Training: hands-on skills, communication skills
