## 综述

![](https://raser-1314796952.cos.ap-beijing.myqcloud.com/The%20Simulation%20Study%20ofGaussian-doped%20Base,%20Abstract.png)
使用ISE软件进行了基于4H-SiC制作的三极管的仿真，根据仿真结果发现在基极使用高斯参杂能够有效得提高器件的电流放大系数。

![](https://raser-1314796952.cos.ap-beijing.myqcloud.com/The%20Simulation%20Study%20ofGaussian-doped%20Base%E4%B8%89%E6%9E%81%E7%AE%A1%E7%BB%93%E6%9E%84.png)
介绍了该三极管的结构

![](https://raser-1314796952.cos.ap-beijing.myqcloud.com/The%20Simulation%20Study%20ofGaussian-doped%20Base%E7%94%B5%E9%98%BB%E7%94%B5%E5%8E%8B%E5%AF%B9%E6%AF%94.png)
文中使用了Constant掺杂与Gaussian掺杂的器件的性能进行了对比

![](https://raser-1314796952.cos.ap-beijing.myqcloud.com/The%20Simulation%20Study%20ofGaussian-doped%20Base%E4%BC%8F%E5%AE%89%E7%89%B9%E6%80%A7%E6%9B%B2%E7%BA%BF.png)
从图里可以明显看出使用了Gaussian掺杂的三极管电流放大的能力明显好于使用Constant掺杂的三极管

![](https://raser-1314796952.cos.ap-beijing.myqcloud.com/The%20Simulation%20Study%20ofGaussian-doped%20Base%E7%94%B5%E6%B5%81%E6%94%BE%E5%A4%A7%E7%B3%BB%E6%95%B0-%E9%9B%86%E7%94%B5%E6%9E%81%E7%94%B5%E6%B5%81.png)
电流放大系数与集电极电流之间的关系，可以明显看出Gaussian掺杂的三极管相比Constant掺杂电流放大的性能

![](https://raser-1314796952.cos.ap-beijing.myqcloud.com/The%20Simulation%20Study%20ofGaussian-doped%20Base%E7%94%B5%E6%B5%81%E6%94%BE%E5%A4%A7%E7%B3%BB%E6%95%B0-%E6%B8%A9%E5%BA%A6.png)
温度对这两种掺杂的三极管电流放大系数都有着明显的影响，但在同样的温度下Gaussian掺杂始终优于Constant掺杂，在高温下Gaussian掺杂的电流放大系数下降明显但仍有一定的电流放大能力。
