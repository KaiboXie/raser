计算耗尽电压
$N_{eff}=\frac{2}{q\epsilon A^2 d(1/C^2)/dV}$ 
$V_{dep}=\frac{q|N_{eff}|d^2}{2\epsilon}=\frac{d^2}{\epsilon ^2 A^2 d(1/C^2)dV}$ 

- 5e13: 
	- $N_{eff}$ = 4.67e14$cm^{-3}$
	- $V_{dep}$ = 4428V
- 3e14: 
	- $N_{eff}$ = 2.27e15$cm^{-3}$
	- $V_{dep}$ = 21561V
- 5e13: 
	- $N_{eff}$ = 3.43e15$cm^{-3}$
	- $V_{dep}$ = 32517V
